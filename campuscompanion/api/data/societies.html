Undergraduate Program - Computer Science and Mathematics Honors Societies

Computer Science Honor Society

Upsilon Pi Epsilon (UPE) is the Computer Science Honor Society. To be eligible, a student must have maintained at least a 3.5 GPA in the first 18 hours of computer science courses as well as an overall GPA of at least 3.0.

For information, contact:
Dr. Barbara Jasmine Adams (Director of Advising) or Dr. Rada Chirkova (UPE Faculty Advisor)

Mathematics Honor Society

Pi Mu Epsilon is the Mathematics Honor Society, to which many computer science students belong. Membership is by invitation only. To be eligible, one must have maintained at least a 3.25 GPA in the first 15 hours of math courses, as well as an overall GPA of at least 3.25. MA 425 or a higher course is required as well.

For information, contact:
Dr. S.O. Paur
Director, Math Honors Program
Department of Mathematics
sopaur@math.ncsu.edu
